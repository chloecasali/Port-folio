About me

Hi ! My name's Chloe and i'm 19 years old. I'm currently a student at OpenClassrooms since August 2022 and I've just finished my four months of Prep'apprentissage, a course divided into three parts with the search of the profession, a project to be carried out with the help of a mentor of the chosen profession, and finally the search for a work-study program (apprenticeship). So, I want to join a company to continue, in a work-study program, my course of web development at OpenClassrooms for a year. In the future, I will also follow a course in UI/UX Design because I'm very interested by that and I would like to learn more of that and in particular, to qualify myself as a full stack web developer and UI/UX designer.

I started be interested in web development when I was very young, thanks to my brother who had created a forum. I remember being very interested in the interface, the layout of the different sections, the modification of our profile and the publication of posts. So I wanted to create too, a forum on animals which was never finished and which I completely gave up. I left this interest for the web a little bit to find my "real profession", not knowing that the web professions existed and were, in fact, emerging more and more. It was in 12th grade that I completely changed paths, moving from journalism to web development, which suited me better. So I did everything to start this retraining as soon as possible, and here I am, creating my port-folio from A to Z and looking for a work-study program in web development.
